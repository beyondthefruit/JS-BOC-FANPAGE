board of canada

biography

Boards of Canada is a Scottish electronic music duo comprised of brothers Mike Sandison and Marcus Eoin. in the 1990s, the duo received recognition following the release of their debut album Music Has the Right to Children in 1998. Their subsequent albums, Geogaddi (2002), The Campfire Headphase (2005) and Tomorrow's Harvest (2013), have received critical praise. They have remained reclusive, rarely giving interviews or performing live.
